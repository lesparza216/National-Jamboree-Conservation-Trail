Ready to Begin Your Adventure?

The National Jamboree Conservation Trail app is available through the Apple App Store for iOS devices. Android users can download the app directly from this website, as it is not currently available through Google Play.

How to Install the Android App

  1. Press the Download Android button above.
  2. Wait for the APK file to finish downloading. You can find it by opening the Downloads folder in Chrome.
  3. Open the downloaded file:
    NSJ26-Conservation-Trail.apk
  4. Android may display a security message about installing apps from outside the Google Play Store.

    Select:
    Settings → Allow from this source
  5. Return to the installation screen and press:
    Install
  6. Once installation is complete, open the app and begin scanning QR codes to collect your badges!

Having Trouble?

  • Installation blocked: Allow your browser or file manager to install apps from outside the Play Store ("Install unknown apps").
  • Parental or device restrictions: Parental controls (such as Google Family Link) or school/work device policies may require approval before installing.
  • Security warnings: Android Play Protect may warn about apps installed outside the Play Store; verify the app source and continue if you trust it.

Note: Allowing Chrome to install apps is only required once. Android will continue protecting your device normally after installation.
